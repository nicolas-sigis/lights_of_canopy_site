**Privacy Policy — Lights of Canopy**

*Last updated: 2026-03-19*

**Developer**:  Nicolas SIGISMONDI

**1. Data Collected**

Lights of Canopy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data about you.

The game locally saves on your device a progress file containing only game data (resources, buildings, narrative progress). This file contains no information that could identify you and is never transmitted to our servers, as we do not have any.

**2. In-App Purchases**

Purchases of optional cosmetic content are processed exclusively by Apple via the StoreKit system. We never have access to your payment information, your Apple ID, or your purchase history. This data is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

**3. iCloud Sync**

If you enable iCloud sync, your progress file is backed up in your personal iCloud space. This sync is managed by Apple and governed by their privacy policy. We have no access to any data stored in your iCloud.

**4. Advertising and Trackers**

Lights of Canopy contains no advertising and integrates no tracking or behavioral analytics tool (no Firebase, no Adjust, no Facebook SDK, etc.).

**5. Minor Users' Data**

The game is rated [PEGI 3 / 4+]. It collects no personal data, including from minor users.

**6. Your Rights (GDPR)**

Since we collect no personal data, there is no data about you that you could request to view, modify, or delete from us.

If you wish to delete your game progress, you can do so directly from the game settings (option "Reset Progress") or by uninstalling the application.

**7. Changes**

Any changes to this policy will be announced via an application update. The date of last update is indicated at the top of this document.

**8. Contact**

For any questions regarding this policy: lightsofcanopy@gmail.com
